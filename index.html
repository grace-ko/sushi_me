ABOUT

Sushi Blossom seats 40 people in a cozy & intimate setting with a bar serving sake, wine & beer, perfect for special as well as casual dining. We also have a dog friendly outdoor patio for fun summer nights!

MENU

We provide an extensive menu of over 200 dishes including mouth watering sashimi, special rolls, bento boxes, seasonal specials, and more, made with the best quality and fresh ingredients.

WATCH US IN ACTION

We work hard to bring you the best Japansese cuisine to enjoy. Free delivery is available in certain areas with a minimum purchase (contact us for details). Come on in for the best sushi in town!

Meet Chase & Esther

Thank you for visiting our website! Sushi Blossom is family owned by Chase & Esther. Chase is a skilled high-end Japanese fusion style sushi chef and Esther has been in the service industry for more than 15 years. Sushi is more than just food to Chase & Esther. It is their passion and the fruit of years of labor & experience - every roll is made special and personal to them; 602 roll for example, is named after Esther's nickname, voted one of customers' favorites. Come meet them and enjoy the best sushi cusine and service in town!
